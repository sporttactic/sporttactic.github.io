Installing the app on a mobile device.

Add and delete a website icon to and from your Home Screen

Iphone/Ipad

Add a website icon to your Home Screen
You can add a website icon to your iPhone Home Screen for quick access to that site.
From the website, tap the Share button, then tap Add to Home Screen.
The icon appears only on the device where you add it.

Delete a website icon from your Home Screen
You can delete a website icon from your iPhone Home Screen. By holding your finger over the icon for a period of time.

The remove symbol appears on the icon. Now click on the remove symbol and remove the icon.

Android/Tablet

Add a website icon to your Home Screen
Touch and hold an app, shortcut, or group.
Slide it to the right until you get a blank Home screen.
Lift your finger.


Delete a website icon from your Home Screen
Tap and hold the icon you wish to delete. A “Remove” icon appears at the bottom of the screen.

Drag the shortcut icon to the “Remove” icon.
